We are building an app that connects individual bakers with customers.

Bakers manage Ingredients, keeping track of their quantities, and compose Products out of Ingredients. Each Product has name, description and picture.

The purpose of the app - Bakers create Posts. Post is an announcement with list of Products with price and quantity, and the time to pick up.

Customers book Products. If Product is out of stock, it cannot be booked. When Customer books something inside Post, a notification is being sent to the Baker. Notifications are in-app for now, without email, etc.

Customers can register only using referal links from Bakers. After registration, the freshly registered Customer is subscribed to the Baker automatically.
